About AXON

AXON
edge of operations

The Name

An axon is the long projection of a nerve cell that transmits signals throughout the body — connecting every part of a living system. AXON the platform does the same for your business: it connects your products, your customers, your expenses, and your profits into one clear, living picture. The gap in the logo — the synapse — represents the spark of insight between your raw numbers and your next smart decision.

The Slogan

"Edge of operations" — AXON sits at the sharp edge where effort becomes money. Built for the shopkeeper, the farmer, the small business owner who works hard and deserves tools that match their hustle.

How It Works

  • Build a profile for your business — shop, farm, company, or supermarket
  • Add all your products, goods, or services with prices and stock levels
  • Record every sale — update quantities, get a transaction ID, see your running revenue
  • Log every expense — rent, wages, stock costs, insurance, taxes, maintenance
  • AXON calculates everything: revenue minus expenses equals your real profit
  • The Analysis page gives you the full picture with suggestions on what to improve

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

AXON is built on one principle: your business data belongs to you alone. We do not collect, sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. Ever.

Local Storage

All data you enter — your business profile, products, sales records, expenses, images — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your own device. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Password Security

Your password is never stored in plain text. It is processed locally and stored in a non-reversible form. No one — including the developer — can recover your password.

Images

Product images you upload are converted to base64 and stored locally in your browser. They are never uploaded to any server.

No Tracking

AXON contains zero analytics scripts, advertising pixels, third-party trackers, or fingerprinting code. The only external connection the app makes is loading fonts from Google Fonts via a standard CDN.

Your Rights

  • You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.
  • You own your data completely and can export it at any time.
